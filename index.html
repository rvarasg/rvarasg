SPECULOOS-3

One of the more exciting projects I have participated on is the descovery of an earh-like planet around the SPECULOOS-3 star. This is a big colaboration around the globe, and together with my colleagues at the IAA and CARMENES data, we were able to help on this study. Our main contribution was to estimate the projected rotational velocity of the star using our CARMENES data. Nevertheless, we have not stopped working on this system, because it is fascinating. We still have more radial velocity data, and we are carefully analysing it.
